S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl

S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl is a gripping first-person shooter set in the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone, where a mysterious second explosion has caused the Zone to become overrun with dangerous anomalies, mutants, and secretive factions. As a "stalker," you venture into this hazardous environment to uncover its secrets, battling other survivors and terrifying creatures along the way. With a blend of intense combat, survival horror, and RPG elements, Shadow of Chernobyl offers a unique open-world experience that is both atmospheric and full of tension.
