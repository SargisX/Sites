The Walking Dead: Season 2

The story continues in The Walking Dead: Season 2, where you now take control of Clementine, the young girl you protected in the first season. As she grows older and more resilient in a world filled with danger, Clementine must survive on her own, facing harsh new threats, including dangerous people and unpredictable walkers. With multiple choices shaping the narrative and a series of heart-wrenching decisions, Season 2 challenges players to face the consequences of their actions while navigating a world where trust is in short supply.
