S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Clear Sky

In S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Clear Sky, return to the Zone as a mercenary in search of answers to the mysterious anomalies that have transformed the area. The prequel to Shadow of Chernobyl, Clear Sky explores the events that led to the Zone's unstable nature. Engage in intense tactical combat, team-based warfare, and uncover secrets about the Zone's origins. The game enhances the gameplay of the original with improved AI, better faction dynamics, and a more refined world filled with danger and mystery.

Price: $18.99

Platform: PC

Release Date: 2008
