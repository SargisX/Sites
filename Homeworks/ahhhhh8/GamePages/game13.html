The Walking Dead: Season 1

Step into the world of The Walking Dead in this critically acclaimed episodic adventure from Telltale Games. In Season 1, players take on the role of Lee Everett, a convicted criminal who finds himself in the midst of a zombie apocalypse. Along with a young girl named Clementine, Lee must navigate the harsh and unforgiving world, making difficult decisions that will affect both his fate and that of others. Featuring a gripping story, emotionally charged moments, and tough moral choices, The Walking Dead: Season 1 is a must-play for fans of narrative-driven games and survival horror.
