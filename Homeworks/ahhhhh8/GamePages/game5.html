Metro Last Light

Step into the boots of Artyom in Metro Last Light, the intense sequel to Metro 2033. The year is 2034, and humanity is teetering on the brink of extinction, living in the dark underground tunnels of Moscow's metro system. A deadly new threat emerges, and Artyom must confront terrifying mutants, rival factions, and a looming global conflict to ensure the survival of the human race. With its rich storytelling, atmospheric environments, and pulse-pounding combat, Metro Last Light is a must-play for fans of survival horror and tactical shooters.
