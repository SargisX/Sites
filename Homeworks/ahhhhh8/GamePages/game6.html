Metro Exodus

Venture beyond the tunnels in Metro Exodus, the third installment of the critically acclaimed Metro series. After escaping the confines of Moscow's metro system, Artyom and his companions embark on a journey across the desolate Russian wasteland in search of a better future. With a vast open-world setting, dynamic weather, and a thrilling narrative, Metro Exodus delivers an epic adventure filled with exploration, stealth, combat, and a gripping story of survival. The game features breathtaking visuals, deep immersion, and a range of choices that will shape Artyom's journey.
