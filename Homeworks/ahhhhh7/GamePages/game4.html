Metro 2033

Based on the novel by Dmitry Glukhovsky, Metro 2033 is a first-person survival horror game set in a post-apocalyptic Russia, where the remnants of humanity survive in the underground metro tunnels of Moscow after a nuclear war. You play as Artyom, a young man who must navigate dark, dangerous tunnels filled with terrifying mutants, hostile factions, and hostile environments. Featuring tense combat, stealth mechanics, and a gripping storyline, Metro 2033 is known for its atmospheric world, survival horror elements, and deep, immersive narrative
