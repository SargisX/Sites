The Walking Dead: A New Frontier

In A New Frontier, the saga continues with both familiar faces and new characters. Players take on the role of Javier Garcia, a former baseball player trying to find his family amidst the chaos of the apocalypse. Alongside Javier is Clementine, now a hardened survivor dealing with the trauma and challenges of the world around her. A New Frontier explores themes of family, loss, and redemption, with choices that directly impact the relationships between characters. The game introduces new dynamics and challenges, including managing the consequences of your decisions and leading a group through a broken world. With a deep narrative and powerful character-driven story, A New Frontier expands the Walking Dead universe in thrilling new directions.

Price: $19.99

Platform: PC

Release Date: 2016
