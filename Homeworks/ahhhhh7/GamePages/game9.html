S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Call of Pripyat

S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Call of Pripyat is the third entry in the series and a direct sequel to Shadow of Chernobyl. Taking place in the Zone after a failed military operation, you play as a Ukrainian special forces officer searching for missing helicopters. Call of Pripyat refines the gameplay, with better AI, improved mechanics, and the exploration of new areas like the infamous Pripyat city. With a focus on survival, exploration, and atmospheric storytelling, this installment further immerses you in the dangerous world of the Zone and its many secrets.

Price: $21.99

Platform: PC

Release Date: 2009
